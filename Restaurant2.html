Shree Guru Kripa

Shree Guru Kripa restaurant is a branch of the very famous joint Guru Kripa for enjoying traditional Indian, pure vegetarian food !! This is very same Guru Kripa, without the noisy & busy environment of the bus stand, and in a very comfortable ambience !! The Shree Guru Kripa restaurant offers pure vegetarian Indian (Punjabi/Awadhi/Mughlai) meals, along with some basic Indo-Chinese snacks !! The restaurant chain is famous for its Indian food, which is of high-quality and offers great value-for-money !! Lunch boxes and thaalis (set Indian meals) also available !! There is ample parking also available at this branch of Guru Kripa !!
