Indore Book Lovers Meet

What we're about:-
 This is a meetup group for anyone interested in books, reading, writing etc. You are an avid reader or a casual one or may be you just started book reading or better still, you want to start it but have been contemplating, well meet the like minded people and get to know fellow book readers of the city. We meet twice a month, every first and third Sunday of the month at 11 AM onwards.
Venue-Reader's Cafe, Palasia, Indore.
