Shreemaya Celebrity

Spacious, fresh, and clean Bakery & Coffee Shop in the names Celebration & Celebrity - by the famous Shreemaya Hotels of Indore !! This one is located at Hotel Shreemaya Residency ! These coffee shops offer high-quality food, prepared hygienically, and quick service !! They specialize in eggless cakes, pastries, puddings, chocolates, cookies & ice creams !! Celebrations & Celebrity also offer fresh breads (multi-grain, sandwich, atta, garlic, etc.), burger buns, paavs, kulchaas, pizza bases, and more !! A huge variety of cookies such as Kaju-Pista, Butter Chocolate, Cake Rusk, Jeera, Ajwain, Mixed Fruit, Chocolate Chip, Nankhatai, etc. are also available !! Muffins, Cream Rolls, Toasts, Rum Balls, Doughnuts, Apple Pie, Pizzas, Sandwiches, Chaat, South Indian Snacks, Chinese platters, Mocktails, Milkshakes, Fruit Juices, Lemon Tea, ice cream Floats, etc. are also on the menu !! They also have a wide selection of products that are pure vegetarian !! Avg. Price/Head: ₹ 200
